Larry DiLullo's Bread

Ingredients

  • 4 Cups All-Purpose Flour
  • 2 1⁄2 Cups Water
  • 1⁄2 Packet Rapid Rise Yeast
  • 1⁄2 Tsp Salt
  • Cast Iron Dutch Oven

Directions

Add flour to liquids and mix.
Cover with Saran Wrap or towel for 12-18 hours.

Wet hands and knockdown risen dough and place it on parchment paper.
Hold the 4 corners of the parchment paper and place it into the pot.
Trim excess parchment paper.
Let it rise for 2 hours.

Set pot in oven and set it for 488 Degrees. (Do not preheat the oven).
Bake for 1 hour and 13 minutes.
